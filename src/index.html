About the Project

The 3D Star Map is an educational tool that allows students to explore the night sky of newly discovered exoplanets. Based on data from the NASA Exoplanet Archive and star catalogs like Gaia DR3, our project visualizes how the sky would look from different perspectives outside of Earth.

The challenge

Develop an app or interface for students that allows them to choose an exoplanet and then either display an interactive star chart or export a high-quality image for printing or viewing on a computer or virtual reality display, where they can draw and name constellations.
